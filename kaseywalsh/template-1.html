About Kasey

A Former Stay-at-Home Mom Who Changed the Science

When your daughter Robbie was diagnosed with AP-4-HSP — a rare neurodegenerative disorder affecting only a handful of families worldwide — you didn't wait for the medical system to catch up. You became the scientist, the connector, and the catalyst the community needed.

Co-founding Cure AP-4 in 2016, you built research collaborations across continents, helped identify a therapeutic research pathway that led to the prediction of FDA-approved medications for off-label use, and expanded disease recognition for patients previously misdiagnosed with cerebral palsy.

Today, as Founder & CEO of Winsights.life, you're channeling 20 years of healthcare service, advocacy, and lived experience into an AI-powered platform that transforms patient and caregiver data into regulatory-grade real-world evidence. Because the science doesn't stop — and neither do you.

The Journey

Every Chapter Counts

2002 – 2006

A Calling in Healthcare

As a Health Care Service Coordinator at the Muscular Dystrophy Association, you served as in-person patient liaison at Boston Children's Hospital, Mass General, and Lahey Clinic — supporting over 600 individuals with neuromuscular diseases.

2016

Co-Founded Cure AP-4

What began as a pledge of hope between two families became a globally recognized nonprofit accelerating research across four AP-4-HSP subtypes — SPG47, SPG50, SPG51, and SPG52.

2016 – 2025

9 Years of Relentless Research

Collaboration with researchers at Boston Children's Hospital, Sheffield University, The Broad Institute, and Cambridge. Biomarker discoveries. Drug predictions. And a community that grew to 230+ families worldwide.

2022

Newburyport Inclusive Playground

Co-founded Newburyport's first inclusive playground initiative, designed for children of all abilities — inspiring a city-wide commitment to inclusive play infrastructure.

2024

Lindamae Lucas Award

Honored by the Commonwealth of Massachusetts for embodying compassion, resilience, advocacy, and humor in special needs parenting and mental health support.

2025 – Present

Founder & CEO, Winsights.life

Launching a mission-driven, AI-powered health tech venture philanthropy to transform lived experience into regulatory-grade insight — and reinvest value into the research that matters most.
