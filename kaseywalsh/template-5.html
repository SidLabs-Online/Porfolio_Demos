About You

Turning Personal Pain Into Public Progress

You are the kind of person who encounters a problem — a daughter with a disease that has no treatment, a city with no inclusive playground, a healthcare system that doesn't collect the data it needs — and builds the solution.

Over the past decade, you co-founded a research nonprofit that now serves 230+ families globally, helped build Newburyport's first inclusive playground (and changed city policy in the process), and identified drug repurposing research that led to the prediction of three FDA-approved medications for potential use in your daughter's disease.

In 2025, you launched Winsights.life — a venture philanthropy using AI and machine learning to transform patient lived experience into regulatory-grade real-world evidence. Because the biggest gap in rare disease research isn't data. It's infrastructure to make that data usable.

The Commonwealth of Massachusetts recognized you with the Lindamae Lucas Award for Excellence in 2024 — for embodying compassion, resilience, advocacy, and humor in the face of life's hardest challenges.

Winsights.life

2025 – Present

AI & ML-powered venture philanthropy transforming patient and caregiver lived experience into regulatory-grade real-world evidence. Building a collaborative ecosystem for patients, families, clinicians, and researchers.

Cure AP-4, Inc.

2016 – 2025

Co-founded grassroots nonprofit that grew from two families into a globally recognized research initiative supporting 230+ families across 4 AP-4-HSP subtypes, with active drug discovery programs underway.

🛝

Newburyport Inclusive Playground

Community · Newburyport, MA

Co-founded the city's first playground designed for children of all abilities. The grassroots effort triggered a city-wide policy commitment: every future park build or equipment replacement will prioritize inclusion.

🤝

Unravel Biosciences Partnership

Research Collaboration

Identified a therapeutic opportunity leading to a Cure AP-4 × Unravel Biosciences collaboration where computational drug discovery predicted 3 FDA-approved medications for AP-4-HSP off-label use. Compounds showed efficacy in disease models.

🏥

Pleozyme Partnership

Research Collaboration

Partnered with Pleozyme Inc. to explore how their emerging therapeutic targeting mitochondrial function and cellular stress might intervene for AP-4-HSP.

📣
