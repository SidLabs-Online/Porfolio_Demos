Your Story

The Woman Behind
the Mission

When your daughter Robbie was diagnosed with AP-4-HSP — a rare, neurodegenerative disorder so uncommon that less than 250 families worldwide carry the diagnosis — you entered the most terrifying and clarifying chapter of your life.

There was no treatment. Barely any research. A handful of families scattered across the globe, each carrying the same impossible weight in total isolation. So you did what communicators do: you built a bridge.

In 2016, you co-founded Cure AP-4, Inc. — a grassroots nonprofit that grew from a pledge between two families into a globally recognized research initiative, funding multiple therapeutic approaches and connecting 230+ families across four disease subtypes. For nine years, you served as the bridge between labs and living rooms — translating research from Sheffield and Cambridge into plain language for exhausted parents across four continents.

Your work contributed to biomarker discoveries, the expansion of disease recognition in misdiagnosed patients, and a groundbreaking drug discovery collaboration with Unravel Biosciences that predicted three FDA-approved medications for potential off-label AP-4-HSP use.

Today, as Founder & CEO of Winsights.life, you're building the platform you always wished existed: a place where the data caregivers generate every single day — the symptoms tracked, the patterns noticed, the observations logged — becomes research-grade evidence that scientists can actually use. Because the story doesn't end with a diagnosis. It's just beginning.
